**Multi-Platform Strategy:**
- [ ] **Cross-posting** — Same content, multiple platforms = multiplied revenue
- [ ] **Platform-native edits** — Optimize formats for each (Shorts 9:16, X 16:9, TikTok trends)

### Revenue Milestones

**Phase 1 (Months 1-6):** Build audience
- Target: 1K YouTube subs, 10K TikTok followers
- Revenue: Affiliate links, small brand deals ($500-1000/month)

**Phase 2 (Months 6-12):** Monetization unlocks
- Target: 10K YouTube subs, 50K TikTok followers
- Revenue: Ad share + affiliates + sponsorships ($2000-5000/month)

**Phase 3 (Year 2):** Sustainable income
- Target: 50K+ YouTube, 200K+ TikTok, 50K+ X followers
- Revenue: Multiple streams ($5000-15000/month)
- Goal: Replace corporate income

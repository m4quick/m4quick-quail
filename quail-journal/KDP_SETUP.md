### Page 3-4: Quick Reference
- Quail incubation: 17-18 days
- Temperature: 99.5°F (forced air) or 100-102°F (still air)
- Humidity: 45-50% first 14 days, 70%+ for lockdown
- Brooder temp: 95°F week 1, reduce 5°F weekly
- Feed: 20-24% protein starter, 18-20% grower

## KDP Upload Steps

1. Go to kdp.amazon.com
2. Sign in with Amazon account
3. Click "Create" → "Paperback"
4. Enter book details
5. Upload manuscript (PDF)
6. Upload cover (PDF)
7. Set pricing
8. Publish!

## Pricing Strategy

- **Cost to print:** ~$2.50-3.50 (KDP calculator)
- **Retail price:** $9.99-12.99
- **Your royalty:** ~$2-4 per book
